## How it all started

BLACKPINK and Selena Gomez's collaboration, ‘Ice Cream’, achieved a significant milestone on April 7, reaching over 900 million views on YouTube. This accomplishment marks the sixth music video by the group to achieve such a feat, joining the ranks of hits like 'DDU-DU DDU-DU', 'Kill This Love', and others. Initially released on August 28, 2020, ‘Ice Cream’ has garnered widespread acclaim for its infectious energy and visually stunning presentation.

The achievement highlights BLACKPINK's unwavering global appeal, captivating audiences worldwide with their electrifying performances and catchy melodies. Surpassing the 900 million-view mark in just over three years, seven months, and nine days since its release underscores the enduring popularity of BLACKPINK and the unwavering support of their dedicated fanbase, known as Blinks.

This milestone reaffirms BLACKPINK's status as one of the music industry's most influential and successful girl groups.
